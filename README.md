??? Food Rescue AI
?? Overview
Food Rescue AI is a smart platform designed to reduce food wastage by connecting surplus food donors with nearby NGOs, shelters, and volunteers. The system helps redistribute excess food before it spoils, ensuring it reaches people in need.
?? Problem
Large amounts of edible food are wasted daily from restaurants, events, and households while many people struggle with hunger. There is often no efficient system to connect food donors with organizations that can distribute this food.
?? Solution
Food Rescue AI provides a platform where donors can register surplus food and NGOs or volunteers can quickly claim and collect it. The system uses intelligent matching to connect donors with nearby receivers, reducing delay and minimizing food waste.
? Key Features
* ?? Register and manage food donations
* ?? View available surplus food
* ?? Location-based matching between donors and NGOs
* ?? Request and claim food donations
* ?? Efficient redistribution of surplus food
